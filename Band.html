HEROES DEL SILENCIO

Héroes del Silencio (sometimes abbreviated Héroes or by their initials HDS) was a Spanish
rock band based in Zaragoza, initially formed by Juan Valdivia (lead guitar)
and Enrique Bunbury (vocals and rhythm guitar), and completed with Joaquín Cardiel (bass)
and Pedro Andreu (drums), in the mid-1980s. They experienced great success in Spain and Latin America,
as well as in several European countries, including Germany, Belgium, Switzerland, France and Italy,
becoming one of the most successful groups in the history of rock in Spanish.
After twelve years in which they sold more than six million records in more than thirty-seven countries,
and played more than a thousand concerts, the band split up in 1996.

Band Members

  • Juan Valdivia (lead guitar)
  • Enrique Bunbury (vocals and rhythm guitar)
  • Joaquín Cardiel (bass)
  • Pedro Andreu (drums)

Most Important Albums

TOUR 2007
It is my favourite album. After ten years of their separation, they started a tour
of 12 concerts between Mexico, Argentina, Guatemala, USA, and Spain. The tour was known as
the farewell tour, as the band split up without saying goodbye. It is my favourite, because it was
the only live performance of the band that I have ever experienced, and every time I listen to it
I can feel and remember all the emotions of that wonderful night.
AVALANCHA
My favourite album, one of the first important live performances of the band in Madrid,
where you can appreciate the quality of their live show.
PARASIEMPRE
Last live performance of the band recorded in Germany, just before the beginning of their first
and only tour in the United States.
